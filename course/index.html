Our programs are divided into two categories - Bootcamps and Courses. We have four categories of courses viz. Business Intelligence (target career role: Business Analyst), Data Analysis (target career role: Data Analyst), Data Engineering (target career role: Data Engineer) and Data Analytics (target career role: AI/ML Engineer). Each course has three difficulty levels: Introduction (101), Intermediate (201) and Advance (301). Learners can choose to specialize in any one or all four categories. A Data Scientist has knowledge of all four sub-disciplines.
